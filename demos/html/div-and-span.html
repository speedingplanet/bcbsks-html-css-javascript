Div and span

The <div> and <span> tags are unstyled elements.

The span is for unstyled in-line content. It does not imply or include a line break or a box.

The div is for unstyled block content. It implies and includes a box. Divs are the width of the viewport by default.

The semantic elements:

  • <main>
  • <header>
  • <footer>
  • <section>
  • <nav>
  • ...and others

act like divs.
